How It Works

  1. User enters a message.
  2. TF-IDF converts the text into numerical features.
  3. The trained Multinomial Naive Bayes model analyzes the message.
  4. The application predicts whether it is Spam or Ham.
  5. The prediction and confidence score are displayed.
  6. The prediction is saved into the SQLite database.
